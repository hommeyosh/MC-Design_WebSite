About Christopher Hoffman

Christopher Hoffman is a multimedia designer, game developer, and entrepreneur. With a passion for digital creativity, he specializes in logos, branding, and web design. Christopher holds an Associate of Science (AS) degree in Multimedia Programming, Simulation, and Gaming from the Community College of Allegheny County (CCAC), along with a business certificate.

He has expertise in Unreal Engine, Blender, Character Creator 4, iClone, HTML, CSS, JavaScript, C++, and C#, using these tools to create stunning 3D animations and interactive experiences. His dedication to design and innovation has driven him to establish MC Design, where he helps businesses bring their visions to life.
